QC score method

QC combines coverage, physical range checks, stability, and recent reporting. Range checks include valid water/discharge values and Type-B sessions where on level should be greater than off level. Flags show why a sensor may need caution, such as long gaps, spikes, flatlines, missing data, or invalid on/off readings.

Criticality score method

Ward criticality combines consumption pressure, groundwater loss, discharge decline, rainfall recovery, and recent CHIRPS rainfall. Higher score means higher concern. Wards with weak sensor confidence are kept as Insufficient Data instead of being forced into a risk category.
